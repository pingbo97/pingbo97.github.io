About the Founder of Inspire
PINGBO ZHOU
Pingbo is a current sophomore at the University of California, Berkeley. He has his fingers crossed very hard for Haas and intends to pursue a double major of Business and Economics.
Pingbo's Story
Born in Guangzhou, China in 1997, Pingbo moved to the United States with his parents when he was three years old.
Growing up in Spokane, Washington, Pingbo always had greater aspirations and believed he would one day make a difference. Upon being accepted to the University of California, Berkeley, he took a few beginner-level coding courses and created Inspire, the premier online photo sharing platform.
Inspire is now the leading photo sharing platform on the web, having already displaced Instagram, Flickr, and Facebook. Its success is unprecedented and the company is looking into going public in the next five years.
